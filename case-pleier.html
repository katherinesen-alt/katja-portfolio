Pleier - audio app for Postimees, one of the largest media groups in the Baltics.

Type Lifestyle, Mobile App, Design Systems
Role Product Designer

Postimees Group brings together dozens of radio stations, podcasts, and media projects.

The content was scattered across different platforms and apps. PLEIER became the single entry point: live broadcasts, show archives, and podcasts from the entire group in one place.

Full product design from scratch in collaboration with a product manager. The work included user flow research, wireframes, final interface in light and dark mode, and a design system that keeps everything consistent across the product and makes future development easier.

The app needed to work equally well for two very different use cases. Passive background listening to live radio, and active search for a specific podcast or episode.

The app launched and is available to users.
