Work with me

A full e-commerce experience for a platform with verified products.

Type E-Commerce, Web Design, MVP
Role Product Designer

Toimib has an unusual model: every product goes through a verification process before it goes on sale. This is not just a marketing claim, it is what the entire user experience is built around. Trust and transparency are not an add-on here, they are the core of the product.

The client came with a rough sketch and a clear business idea but no design foundation. The work covered the full customer journey from the homepage to checkout: a catalog with category and price filters, product pages with video reviews and ratings, a slide-out cart, a multi-step checkout with card and bank payment options, a wishlist, gift cards, and an about page explaining the verification process.

Every screen was designed around one idea: this store is different because it actually checks what it sells.
