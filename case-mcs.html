Spaceit MCS – Mission Control Software. Full product design of a cloud-based satellite mission control platform.

Type SaaS, Complex Systems, Design System
Role Product Design

Satellite operators were managing missions manually – telemetry in CSV files, commands in Google Docs, no alerts, no queue. The existing tool was built by developers and barely covered the basics.

The dashboard needed to be fully customizable – operators had different satellites with different parameters, and a fixed layout would have served no one. I took an existing idea that wasn't working and redesigned it into a flexible widget system.

Dark mode was non-negotiable for the context – people monitoring satellite data work in low-light environments around the clock.

User interviews revealed that operators need quick access to critical functions on the go – but not everything. I made the deliberate decision to leave out non-essential features rather than shrink the desktop into a phone.

A custom design system keeps everything consistent – from colors and typography to components and layouts, making it easy to scale and maintain across the entire platform.

The platform launched and is now used by real Spaceit clients for live satellite mission management.
